Attending the Annual Meeting
The 2023 Annual Shareholders Meeting will be held as a virtual-only meeting.
Any shareholder can join the Annual Meeting, while shareholders of record as of September 29 2023, will be able to vote and submit questions during the meeting.
Date: Thursday, December 7, 2023
Time: 8:30 a.m. Pacific Time
Virtual Shareholder Meeting:
www.virtualshareholdermeeting.com/MSFT23
Submit Your Question
We invite you to submit any questions via the proxy voting site at
www.proxyvote.com
. We will include as many of your questions as possible during the Q&A session of the meeting and will provide answers to questions on the Microsoft Investor Relations website under the Annual Meeting page.
